I'm an Astra Fellow at [Redwood Research](https://redwoodresearch.org/), working on AI safety. My research is motivated by understanding and reducing risks from increasingly capable AI systems.

In previous lives I...

- Was a post-doc at the Alan Turing Institute on [Project Bluebird](https://www.turing.ac.uk/science-innovation/defence-and-national-security/project-bluebird), working on synthetic scenario generation for autonomous air-traffic control.
- Did a PhD in mathematical physics at the University of Oxford, with a thesis on *[Generalized Symmetries in String Theory Realizations of Quantum Field Theories](https://www.maths.ox.ac.uk/node/64281)*.
