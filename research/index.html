  1. Deep Learning for Multi-Label Disease Classification of Retinal Images: Insights from Brazilian Data for AI Development in Lower-Middle Income Countries
    Dewi S. W. Gould, Jenny Yang, and David A. Clifton
    medRxiv, 2024
    Abs DOI

    Retinal fundus imaging is a powerful tool for disease screening and diagnosis in opthalmology. With the advent of machine learning and artificial intelligence, in particular modern computer vision classification algorithms, there is broad scope for technology to improve accuracy, increase accessibility and reduce cost in these processes. In this paper we present the first deep learning model trained on the first Brazilian multi-label opthalmological datatset. We train a multi-label classifier using over 16,000 clinically-labelled fundus images. Across a range of 13 retinal diseases, we obtain frequency-weighted AUC and F1 scores of 0.92 and 0.70 respectively.
